Chinese Noodle Soup

Description

Authentic-ish chinese noodle soup that you don't have to go to China for it!

Ingredients:

  • Broth:
    • 3 cup chicken broth, low sodium.
    • 2 smashed garlic cloves
    • 1/2" sliced into 3 ginger
    • 1 1/2 tbsp light soy sauce
    • 2 tsp sugar
    • 1 1/2 tbsp chinese cooking wine (dry sherry)
    • 1/4 - 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • boiling water.
  • 1 large finely diced shallot.
  • 4 chopped garlic cloves.
  • 1 tsp chopped ginger (optional).
  • 2 whisked eggs, seasoned with salt (optional)
  • 6-8 oz tofu, or any meat.
  • salt and pepper to taste.
  • 2 tbsp oil (peanut, wok, or coconut).
  • 1 lime.

Steps

  1. Boil pad thai noodles until soften, then drain and spray with cold water.
  2. Pad Thai sauce: Mix soy sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar together. Set aside.
  3. Blot tofu with paper towels, cut into cubes, then stir fry in pan with oil until edges are slightly golden. Add other proteins as desired.
  4. In same pan, add the shallot, garlic, ginger and stir fry
  5. In the same pan, clear a section and add the whisked eggs, stir until cooked and in little pieces.
  6. Add pad thai noodles and pad thai sauce. Stir on medium to low heat until thoroughly combined.
