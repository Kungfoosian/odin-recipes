Vietnamese Stir Fry Macaroni with Ground Beef

Description

A favorite dish of mine growing up, very simple to create!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup ground beef
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup sliced onion
  • chopped cilantro
  • black pepper
  • 1/3 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp rice cooking wine
  • 1 cup pasta (any kind)

Steps

  1. Boil your pasta until soften, about 7 minutes. Once done, dain and rinse with cold water. Set aside.
  2. Heat up a pan. Add oil and the sliced onion and sautee for a few minutes
  3. Add ground beef , stir until slightly cooked. Add rice cooking wine and ketchup .
  4. Add garlic powder and sugar , continue stirring. Add cooked pasta then stir for a few minutes on medium to low heat.
  5. Add soy sauce, stir and add some black pepper. Turn off heat.
  6. Add some chopped cilantro and serve.
