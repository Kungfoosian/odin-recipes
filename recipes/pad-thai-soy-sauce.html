Pad Thai (with soy sauce!)

Description

Another favorite dish of mine that I didn't discover until I was 18! This dish is usually made with fish sauce, but if you or someone you're making this for is allergic to seafood, then this recipe is for you (and them) !

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz pad thai noodles.
  • boiling water.
  • 1 large finely diced shallot.
  • 4 chopped garlic cloves.
  • 1 tsp chopped ginger (optional).
  • 2 whisked eggs, seasoned with salt (optional)
  • 6-8 oz tofu, or any meat.
  • salt and pepper to taste.
  • 2 tbsp oil (peanut, wok, or coconut).
  • 1 lime.
